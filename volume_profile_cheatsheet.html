Key Levels — Know What You're Looking At

POC · Point of Control

Price with the most volume. Acts as a magnet — price keeps returning. Strong support from above, resistance from below.

VAH · Value Area High

Upper bound of where 70% of volume traded. Resistance when inside the area, support when above.

VAL · Value Area Low

Lower bound of the value area. Support when inside, resistance when below.

Full Workflow — Every Trade

1.
WHERE · Horizontal Profile POC / VAH / VAL — is price at a key level?
2.
WHEN · Vertical Volume Is there a real volume spike, or weak participation?
3.
WHO · Buy% / Sell% Who is in control at that row — aggressive buyers or sellers?

✓ Pre-Entry Checklist

  1. Where am I relative to POC / VAH / VAL?
  2. Buy%/Sell% at this row — dominant side?
  3. Vertical volume — spike or flat?
  4. Virgin level or retested?
  5. Aligned with HTF trend / BB / EMAs?
3+ aligned → take it. 1-2 → pass.

✗ Don'ts

  • Don't trade the first 30-60 min (profile too thin)
  • Don't fade a breakout with high aggression + volume
  • Don't ignore HTF trend — profile works best with bias
  • Don't chase moves already past VAH/VAL without a pullback
  • Don't trust POC on day 1 — it evolves, peaks mid-session
